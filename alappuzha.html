Alappuzha - Venice of The East.


Alappuzha, also known by its former name Alleppey, is the administrative headquarters of Alappuzha district in the Indian state of Kerala. Alleppey is a city and a municipality in Kerala with an urban population of 174,164 and ranks third among the districts in literacy rate in the state. In 2016, the Centre for Science and Environment rated Alappuzha as the cleanest town in India. Alappuzha is considered to be the oldest planned city in this region and the lighthouse built on the coast of the city is the first of its kind along the Laccadive Sea coast.

Popular Destinations


Stay in House Boat

A vacation to Kerala is incomplete without spending some relaxing time, cruising through the backwaters of Alleppey. You can sit back and relax as you enjoy the magnificent setting of beautiful coconut fringed waters, paddy fields and human-made islands.

Ambalappuzha Sree Krishna Temple

Ambalapuzha Sree Krishna Temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna.This temple is Built-in the traditional Kerala style architectural pattern, the temple is famous for its delicious rice pudding, popularly known as Pal Payasam.

Marari Beach

One of the great delights of the Malabar Coast, the Marari Beach is an idyllic serene beach. About 11 km from the city of Allappuzha, this beach is a hotspot for fishing activity, The serenity and quietude on the beach are beyond description.
